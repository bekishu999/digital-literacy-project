# UPI Fraud Awareness

UPI fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India today.  
It has increased rapidly with the rise of digital payment systems.  

## How UPI Fraud Happens

In this type of fraud, scammers trick users into sharing sensitive banking details such as OTPs or approving payment requests.  

A typical situation begins when a fraudster contacts the victim.  
- They may pretend to be a bank official or customer support agent.  
- Sometimes, they act as buyers from online platforms.  

The scammer may send a fake link or a payment request.  
They tell the victim that approval is needed to receive money.  
However, when the victim approves the request, money is deducted instead of being credited.  

## Who Gets Targeted

Students and individuals with less awareness are often targeted.  
Once an OTP is shared or a request is approved, the transaction happens instantly and cannot be reversed easily.  

This results in:  
- Financial loss  
- Stress  
- Loss of trust  

Many victims also find it difficult to recover their money.  

## Prevention Tips

This case highlights the importance of digital awareness.  
Users should:  
- Never share OTPs or approve unknown requests  
- Verify links carefully  
- Use trusted applications only

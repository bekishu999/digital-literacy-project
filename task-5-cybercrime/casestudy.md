**UPI fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India.**  

**It has increased rapidly with the growth of digital payments.**  



**In this type of fraud, scammers trick users into sharing**  

**sensitive banking details like OTPs or approving payment requests.**  



**A common situation starts when a fraudster contacts the victim.**  

**They pretend to be a bank official, customer support agent,**  

**or even a buyer from an online platform.**  



**The scammer may send a fake link or a payment request.**  

**They tell the victim to approve it to receive money.**  

**However, once the request is approved, money gets deducted**  

**instead of being credited.**  



**Students and unaware users are often targeted.**  

**Once an OTP is shared or a request is approved,**  

**the transaction happens instantly and is hard to reverse.**  



**This leads to financial loss, stress, and loss of trust**  

**in digital platforms.**  



**This case highlights the importance of digital awareness.**  

**Users should never share OTPs, avoid suspicious links,**  

**and always use trusted applications for transactions.**

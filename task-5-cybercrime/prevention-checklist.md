#### **Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**



* &#x20;**Never share your OTP with anyone**
* &#x20;**Do not approve unknown payment requests**
* &#x20;**Always verify links before clicking**
* &#x20;**Use official banking apps only**
* &#x20;**Enable two-factor authentication**
* &#x20;**Avoid using public WiFi for transactions**
* &#x20;**Regularly check your bank statements**
* &#x20;**Keep your apps and phone updated**
* &#x20;**Do not trust unknown calls claiming to be from banks**
